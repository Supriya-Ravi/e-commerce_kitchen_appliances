Who We Are

Launched in 2006, CookEasy Kitchen was the first one-of-its kind large format specialist retail store that catered to all multi-brand kitchen appliances and kitchen needs in India. Over a decade since its inception, CookEasy has almost become synonyms for all kitchen appliances and electronics needs, with its tech-savvy staff, product range, online presence and the will to help customers.
